ABOUT

Know more about the event

The International Blockchain Olympiad (IBCOL) is an annual global competition inviting current and recent students to propose solutions for real-world problems with blockchain technology which is a multidisciplinary design competition that invites students to apply blockchain and cryptography for solving real-world challenges. The purpose of this event is to nurture the next generation of blockchain professionals with decentralized applications, built with blockchain or distributed ledger technology.
